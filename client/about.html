About Our Journey

Globel prime Overseas is a global manpower agency that provides international welding services and recruits workers for businesses in Europe and the Gulf states. Our first concern is the interests and priorities of the client by providing them the best manpower resources.

We hire and provide only competent workers who are reliable and hardworking. Our team is well-experienced, innovative, and dedicated to their work. We are the top among recruitment agencies in Europe for foreigners, and we find jobs for our clients based on their preferences and the best of arranged facilities; as we strictly adhere to European law and regulations.

Global prime wants to embody the scale of balance between employers and employees. Moreover, we aim to lead as a role model for future companies to such standards. We value justice, rights, and ethics, and we aspire to see a fair and peaceful world for everyone. “There can be no justice without peace, and there can be no peace without justice.” .
